# Hero Skills — the complete tree, every option

This chapter documents **all 30 skills at every rank with every
subskill option**, verified in full from the official Skills Overview.
Format per skill: the core Basic → Advanced → Expert progression, then
the **Advanced picks** (choose one when reaching Advanced) and
**Expert picks** (choose one at Expert). "×2 w/ X" marks a synergy
clause: *that subskill's bonus doubles if the hero also knows skill X*
— the full synergy web is mapped after the trees.

## How the system works
Level-up = one random attribute point + a choice of three options
(new skill or rank-up). Every skill: **Basic → Advanced (pick 1 of 3
subskills) → Expert (pick 1 of 3 stronger subskills).** Picks are
permanent — except via Relic artifacts, which grant *all* unlearned
subskills of specific trees (see the artifacts section below).
**Combat** is Might-hero exclusive; **Thaumaturgy** is Magic-hero
exclusive; the six faction skills auto-belong to their factions.

## Might Skills

### Offense — *the damage multiplier*
Basic **+10%** → Advanced **+15%** → Expert **+20%** damage on all
friendly basic attacks.
- **Advanced picks:** Archery (+15% Ranged/Long Reach damage) ·
  Battle March (+1 Focus Point per attack, ×2 w/ Luck) · Battle
  Frenzy (+2 Attack per point of Morale)
- **Expert picks:** Shadow Blades (+1 flat damage — best on
  swarm/multi-attack armies, worst on giants) · Reality Wardens
  (+50% damage vs. summoned creatures, ×2 w/ Nightshade — the
  anti-Schism/anti-summon capstone) · Firmness (−15% enemy Defense)
- **Play it:** the default first Might pick for every faction. Archery
  for shooter cores (Temple, Grove, Dungeon); Battle Frenzy for
  morale builds; Battle March for Focus economies (Hive, Grove).

### Defense — *the damage divider*
Basic **−10%** → Advanced **−15%** → Expert **−20%** damage taken
from basic attacks.
- **Advanced picks:** Cover (−20% from Ranged/Long Reach) · Hymn to
  the Martyrs (+1 Focus Point when a creature takes damage, ×2 w/
  Recruitment — the tank-battery) · As Luck Would Have It (+2 Defense
  per point of Luck)
- **Expert picks:** Shields and Shells (enemy −1 flat damage —
  mirrors Shadow Blades: crushing vs. swarms) · Wizard Contract (−10%
  Magic Damage, ×2 w/ Diplomacy) · Unstoppable Force (−15% enemy
  Attack)
- **Play it:** Temple's and Grove's wall builds; Cover is the
  counter-pick when the enemy is the shooter faction.

### Leadership — *the morale engine*
Hero gains **+1 → +2 → +3 Morale** (army-wide via the hero).
- **Advanced picks:** Resolve (+1% extra-turn chance per Morale
  point, ×2 w/ Battlecraft — the core of the morale-stack ceiling) ·
  Where the Sun Never Sets (+2 Atk/Def/SP/sight in areas you *don't*
  control, ×2 w/ Daylight — the invader's pick) · Hero of Legends
  (enemy −1 Morale)
- **Expert picks:** Inspiring Strike (5% per Morale point to
  instantly reset Heroic Strike on use) · March! (all friendly
  creatures +1 Initiative) · Enthusiasm (any morale-granted turn →
  whole army +1 Morale until next round — morale that compounds
  itself)
- **Play it:** useless for Undead/Construct armies (Morale locked at
  0 — see combat appendix); build-defining for Temple, Hive, Grove.
  Aeos the Exalted is this tree as a hero.

### Luck — *the crit engine*
Hero gains **+1 → +2 → +3 Luck**.
- **Advanced picks:** Beastly Confidence (+1% Lucky Strike chance per
  Luck point, ×2 w/ Primal Magic) · Always Heads (+25% gold/resources
  from map piles, ×2 w/ Economy) · Mearea's Chosen (enemy −1 Luck)
- **Expert picks:** Lucky Hit (Lucky Strikes provoke **no
  counterattack**) · Lightning Strikes Twice (**spells** can Lucky
  Strike for 200% damage, +5% chance per Luck — casters, read that
  twice) · Lucky Day (a Lucky Strike gives the whole army +1 Luck
  until next round)
- **Play it:** Grove's Qilins (native Luck 3) and hero Octavia make
  this a faction signature; Lightning Strikes Twice quietly makes
  Luck a *caster* skill.

### Resistance — *the anti-mage wall*
Friendly creatures take **−15% → −25% → −35% Magic Damage**.
- **Advanced picks:** Obstruction (enemy spells +20% mana; +20% more
  for a school you know) · Hindrance Aura (enemy loses 1 Focus Charge
  per round after the first) · Fast Metabolism (debuffs on you last
  −2 rounds)
- **Expert picks:** Magic Suppression (granted ability: heavy Magic
  Damage to a summoned creature, scaling with level) · Time Shift
  (+1 round to all enemy spell cooldowns) · Draining (enemy **−1
  level on all Magic School spells**)
- **Play it:** the caster-matchup sideboard. Draining vs. enemy
  school-masters; Magic Suppression as the third anti-summon tool.

### Tactics — *the deployment war*
Placement zone **3 → 4 → 5 lines**; enemy Tactics-knowers lose
**1 → 2 → 3 lines** of their own zone.
- **Advanced picks:** One for All (+2 Attack per adjacent enemy, ×2
  w/ Offense) · Easy Prey (once/round, enemy waits → your army +2
  Initiative) · Energizing Mana (+1 Focus Charge when the enemy
  casts)
- **Expert picks:** All for One (+2 Defense per adjacent ally, ×2 w/
  Defense — phalanx formations) · Riposte Mastery (a counterattack
  kill refreshes that stack's counterattacks) · Spellcaster Tactics
  (Focus spends grant the hero 4 temporary mana)
- **Play it:** the only directly adversarial skill — in
  hero-vs-hero metas it's near-mandatory; Kel'Ghul starts with it.

### Battlecraft — *the wait/skip economy*
Waiting grants **+20% → +30% → +40% Attack** until round's end;
skipping grants the same in **Defense**.
- **Advanced picks:** Melee Mastery (±10% melee dealt/taken) ·
  Ranged Mastery (±10% ranged dealt/taken) · Overwatch (waiting
  units counterattack enemies entering melee range)
- **Expert picks:** Battle Focus (+1 Focus Charge per round after
  the first, ×2 w/ Battle Magic) · Preemptive Strike (skipping units
  counter *before* the enemy's blow) · Manacraft (+2 max mana per
  point of hero Attack+Defense, ×2 w/ Recruitment — the Might-caster
  bridge)
- **Play it:** the skill that makes Wait a weapon; doubles Resolve
  via the synergy clause, so morale builds want it twice over.

### Siegecraft — *the gate-breaker*
Catapult **+50 → +100 → +150 damage**.
- **Advanced picks:** Relentless Assault (+1 Speed/Initiative while
  besieging) · Forward Observers (walls don't reduce your ranged
  damage) · Barrage (new siege ability: hit a hex and all adjacent,
  1/round)
- **Expert picks:** Phalanx (−50% damage from defender towers) ·
  Tunnelling (teleport a friendly unit 3 hexes, 1/round) · Sabotage
  (**the enemy gate is always open**)
- **Play it:** pairs with Earth's Rage (Primal) for the complete
  siege kit; Sabotage + fast melee is a castle-cracking build unto
  itself.

### Recruitment — *the growth skill*
**+4 T1 → +3 T2 → +2 T3** weekly growth in all cities (cumulative).
- **Advanced picks:** Direct Supervision (hero in a city at week
  start → that city +50% growth) · Elite Guards (T1–3 creatures gain
  25% of hero Atk/Def, ×2 w/ Tactics) · Veterans (+2 T4 growth, ×2
  w/ Diplomacy)
- **Expert picks:** Relocation (grants the map spell — see Neutral
  Magic chapter) · Strenuous Training (all friendlies +2 HP) ·
  Mentors (**upgrade creatures outside cities** if the army holds an
  upgraded copy, at a premium)
- **Play it:** the swarm factions' economy (Onkos + this = skeleton
  tsunami; Hive Progenitor chasers pass through here); Mentors
  removes the march-home-to-upgrade tax entirely.

### Combat *(Might-hero exclusive)* — *the Heroic Strike tree*
Heroic Strike **+10 → +15 → +20 base damage**.
- **Advanced picks:** Effortless Strike (−1 Focus Charge cost) ·
  Revenge (once/round, a friendly stack's death → extra Heroic
  Strike) · Mighty Strike (+10% damage per hero level)
- **Expert picks:** Swordcraft (+2 Attack and Defense) · Battle
  Thrill (a Heroic Strike **kill** → use it again) · Confusing
  Strike (Heroic Strike strips 1 counterattack)
- **Play it:** Old Lord Mandall's tree; Battle Thrill chains
  executions across wounded stacks — pair with Coup de Grâce logic.

## Magic Skills

### Battle Magic — *stats from Spellpower*
Friendly Atk/Def gain **15% → 25% → 35%** of the hero's Spellpower
and Knowledge respectively.
- **Advanced picks:** Aura of Destruction / Protection / Wizardry
  (each cast in battle → hero +1 Attack / Defense / Spellpower until
  battle's end — pick your snowball stat)
- **Expert picks:** Battle Mage's Authority (+20% of SP as army
  Attack, ×2 w/ Sorcery) · Mage Protector's Authority (+20% of
  Knowledge as army Defense, ×2 w/ Resistance) · Magic Time (all
  battle-spell cooldowns −1 round)
- **Play it:** the skill that makes a caster's stats feed the army —
  Vesper starts with it; the Righteousness interaction is filthy
  (every stack of +SP also buffs the army through this).

### Sorcery — *raw magic damage*
**+10% → +20% → +30% Magic Damage dealt.**
- **Advanced picks:** Summoning Sorcery (all your battle summons
  +25% HP and damage) · Magic Arrow (grants the free, no-cooldown
  nuke as a subskill) · Raw Energy (+10% more, ×2 w/ Offense)
- **Expert picks:** Piercing Spells (ignore 25% of magic resistance)
  · High Mage (**+1 level to all spells of schools you know**) ·
  Sticky Magic (your effects last +2 rounds, ×2 w/ Wisdom)
- **Play it:** the universal caster multiplier — every damage number
  in the magic chapters rises 30% at Expert. Laura starts with it.

### Summon Avatar — *the free champion*
Grants the Avatar summon; it survives **2 → 3 → 4 hits**; its
Attack/Damage scale with Spellpower. (Full formulas: Neutral Magic
chapter.)
- **Advanced picks:** Avatar of Fury (+25% damage, ×2 w/ Leadership)
  · Avatar of Toughness (+1 hit, ×2 w/ Defense) · Avatar of Celerity
  (+1 Speed/Initiative, ×2 w/ Insight)
- **Expert picks:** Stabilization (**your summons become immune to
  summon-dispel effects** — the answer to Anti-Magic/Heavenly
  Blades) · Their Name is Legion (+30% to all your summon counts) ·
  Fields of Mana (summon onto *any* free hex)
- **Play it:** Funerella starts with it; Stabilization single-handedly
  rehabilitates every summon build this book warned about — Schism
  players, this is your tech.

### Wisdom — *the school-skipper*
Learn spells up to **T3 → T4 → T5** *without* the school skill.
- **Advanced picks:** Rite of Magic (convert current Movement into
  mana) · Between the Lines (learn high Neutral magic regardless of
  hero level) · Seeing Through (Heroic Strike kills all temporary
  units in the target)
- **Expert picks:** Rhythmic Cadence (+30% max mana, ×2 w/ Sorcery)
  · Eagle Eye (**at battle start, learn all learnable enemy
  spells**) · Stargazer (+250 Astrology Points/day, ×2 w/ Scouting)
- **Play it:** the generalist-caster spine — cherry-pick the best
  spell from every school (Vulnerability, Rewind Life, Web) without
  four school skills. Zenith starts with it.

### The four School skills (Daylight / Nightshade / Arcane / Primal)
Shared shape — Basic: learn that school to T5 · Advanced: **school
spells +1 level** · Expert: **+1 more level**, and T5 spells
learnable without a Mage Guild (via the Observatory).
- **Shared picks:** [School] Time (−1 round on that school's
  cooldowns, Advanced) · Brightest Sun / Darkest Night / Purest
  Thought / Sharpest Teeth (**−1 level to all enemy spells of that
  school**, Advanced) · [School] Teachings (+1 further level,
  Expert) · **Scholar Synergy: Arina/Naira/Doreath/Hksmilla** (+6
  Spellpower for that school, **+3 more per other school known**,
  Expert)
- **Unique picks:** Daylight — Luminous Focus (+1 Focus Charge at
  battle start) and Speed of Light (+10 Movement, ×2 w/ Logistics).
  Nightshade — Chronic Weakness (enemy max HP −1, ×2 w/ Resistance)
  and Hour of the Wolf (enemy −10% Atk/Def). Arcane — All Life is
  Endless (+30% healing/revival, ×2 w/ Summon Avatar — Rewind Life's
  best friend) and Mana Flex (spells −2 mana). Primal — Primal Force
  (your effects +1 round) and Chaos Reigns (+50% Lucky Strike
  damage, ×2 w/ Battle Magic).
- **Play it:** **spell levels stack** — Advanced (+1) + Expert (+1) +
  Teachings (+1) reaches L4 on the whole school from the skill tree
  alone; add High Mage/Archmage/Fine Wand and even fresh scrolls cast
  at max. The Scholar Synergy capstone is the multi-school payoff:
  a four-school hero casts each school at +15 effective Spellpower.

### Thaumaturgy *(Magic-hero exclusive)* — *the double-cast*
Cast a **second spell each round** from a school unused that round;
extra casts cost **+100% → +75% → +50%** mana.
- **Advanced picks:** Antimage (enemy spells +4 mana) · Ancient
  Scrolls (all Tier-1 spells added to the book) · Practical
  Incantations (Heroic Strike +10)
- **Expert picks:** Thaumaturge Brilliance (+2 Spellpower and
  Knowledge) · Vast Soul (+10% mana each morning) · Archmage (**+1
  level to all known-school spells**)
- **Play it:** the reason to be multi-school — Vulnerability +
  Fireball in the same round is a different game. Anastasia starts
  with it; Lord Rufus's Thaumaturgy start means Masterful Rewind
  Life *plus* a second cast.

## General Skills

### Diplomacy — *armies for gold*
Neutral squads may **offer to join for gold**; Persuasion **+0% →
+25% → +50%**.
- **Advanced picks:** Larger Than Life (army looks +25% stronger to
  neutrals, ×2 w/ Battlecraft) · Eloquence (−75% surrender cost) ·
  Folk Hero (+50% Persuasion vs. same-faction neutrals)
- **Expert picks:** Art of the Deal (−15% joining cost, ×2 w/
  Economy) · Vagrant Army (neutral-affinity units gain +100% of hero
  Atk/Def) · Negotiator (+20% joiners)
- **Play it:** Grove's gold-cost escape hatch (Alluring Sh'a's
  tree); King-of-Kings starts with it. Vagrant Army turns recruited
  neutrals from filler into elites.

### Logistics — *the movement skill*
**+10% → +15% → +20% Movement.**
- **Advanced picks:** Leaps and Bounds (+5 Movement per battle
  victory) · Strong Mounts (Neutral map spells −4 mana, ×2 w/
  Scouting) · Careful Planning (bank 50 unused Movement daily, ×2 w/
  Arcane)
- **Expert picks:** Secret Passages (terrain penalties −50%) · Back
  to Town! (grants the map spell) · Battle Logistics (+1 Speed on
  Native Terrain)
- **Play it:** the Foundations chapter's "time is the real currency"
  as a skill. Ister and Marl start with it.

### Scouting — *the intel skill*
Hero sight **+1 → +2 → +3** (and sight multiplies half the map-spell
ranges in this book).
- **Advanced picks:** Long-Sight (+1 more, ×2 w/ Luck) · Familiar
  Paths (+10 Movement starting in controlled territory, ×2 w/
  Wisdom) · Pathfinding (terrain penalties −50%)
- **Expert picks:** Reconnaissance (defog 150% of sight each
  morning) · Visions (**exact enemy army compositions** within 300%
  of sight — Read Minds as a passive) · Counterespionage (enemies
  see *fake* info about your army)
- **Play it:** Visions makes fight selection free forever;
  Counterespionage is the PvP mind-game pick.

### Insight — *the compounding skill*
**+20% → +30% → +40% XP.**
- **Advanced picks:** Enlightenment (+10% more XP) · Civic
  Innovation (**+30% Law points**, ×2 w/ Leadership — the town-Laws
  accelerator) · Sagacity (neutral intentions within 300% sight)
- **Expert picks:** Scholar of Magic (**heroes teach each other
  spells** when they meet on the map) · Endless Charisma (+1 to all
  four attributes, ×2 w/ Tactics) · Perception (see enemy hero
  details in battle)
- **Play it:** Pip's tree, and the multi-hero economy's secret
  weapon — one Scholar of Magic hero turns every spell any hero
  learns into a spell *every* hero learns.

### Economy — *gold as a skill*
**+500 → +750 → +1,000 gold daily.**
- **Advanced picks:** Tax Collector (+250 more, ×2 w/ Logistics) ·
  Smuggler (+1 of your faction's rare resource daily) · Master
  Builder (+1 Wood and Ore daily)
- **Expert picks:** Experimenter (**+5 Alchemical Dust daily**, ×2 w/
  Insight — the artifact-upgrade economy) · Merchant (hero counts as
  an extra Marketplace for trade rates) · Antiquarian (sell
  artifacts +25%, buy −25%)
- **Play it:** the second-hero skill par excellence (Clarissa,
  Milossa); Smuggler answers the T7 rare-resource gate from inside
  the skill tree.

## Faction Skills (complete trees)

### Righteousness (Temple)
Per friendly death **or** kill: hero +1 to all four attributes for
**2 → 3 → 4 rounds**, stackable.
- **Advanced picks:** Fields of Serenity (native-terrain ±10%) ·
  Righteous Sacrifice (each trigger → 3 temporary mana) · Purging
  Touch (granted ability: dispel all debuffs from a target)
- **Expert picks:** Guardian (**a friendly creature that kills fully
  restores its HP**) · The Chosen One (granted ability: buff a
  creature by 50% of hero Atk+SP / Def+Knowledge) · Strong Faith
  (each trigger → +1 more to everything until round's end)
- **Read:** Guardian on a lifedrain-less faction is enormous —
  Temple's melee stops attritting the moment it starts winning.

### Necromancy (Necropolis)
Converts **10% → 15% → 20%** of the strongest enemy stack into
same-tier Undead; Energy caps **1,000 → 1,250 → 1,500**. *(Corrects
the flat-20% figure in this book's earlier draft — 20% is the Expert
rank.)*
- **Advanced picks:** Gravesoil (terrain) · Soul Harvest (+4 mana
  per victory) · Death Herald (+500 max Energy)
- **Expert picks:** Soul Reaper (enemy −1 Morale **and** Luck) ·
  Eternal Service (granted ability: heal/revive a friendly Undead
  stack) · Fields of the Dead (+50% Energy gain when you lack the
  tier)
- **Read:** Eternal Service is Rewind Life as an ability — Rufus
  players get both.

### Murmuring (Grove)
**1 → 2 → 3 Focus Charges** at every battle's start.
- **Advanced picks:** Mycelium Grounds (terrain) · Life Essence (+1
  Focus cap) · Child of the Woods (+20 max mana)
- **Expert picks:** Just One More Time (Focus spends cut hero spell
  cooldowns −2 rounds) · Burst of Energy (Focus spends → Heroic
  Strike +15% per hero level) · Strong Connection (Focus spends →
  hero +2 all attributes)

### Summon Swarm (Hive)
Eggs → Fire Larvae at **8% → 12% → 16%** of army Hive-Spawn HP;
scales with hero level; eggs die to 2 hits.
- **Advanced picks:** Warmth of the Nest (terrain) · Thy Children
  (+1 Larva per hero level) · Ravage (Larvae +1 damage)
- **Expert picks:** Hive Power (hero +1 Luck and Morale) · Hardened
  Shells (eggs +1 hit) · Hive Flame (Larvae last +1 round)

### Abyssal Communion (Schism)
Victory → Communion +1 (cap **4 → 5 → 6**); each level = **+3%
friendly Schism units** for that battle; **halves every morning**.
- **Advanced picks:** Our True Home (terrain) · Comprehensible
  Depths (+1 max level) · Call of the Void (each morning, 3 mana per
  level *before* halving)
- **Expert picks:** Black Ice (enemy spells +1 mana per active
  level) · **Abyssopelagial (+1 extra Communion per victory —
  doubles the climb rate)** · Celestial Abyss (+1% units per level)
- **Read:** Abyssopelagial is the faction's most important subskill,
  full stop — it halves the streak-building the whole gameplan
  depends on.

### Triumvirate's Strength (Dungeon)
Once/round, activate a Stance: hero **+2 → +4 → +6** to that
attribute.
- **Advanced picks:** Underground Passages (terrain) · Alvarian
  Expertise (active Stance also *reduces* the enemy hero's matching
  attribute) · Underground Mana Pools (3 temporary mana per Stance
  pick)
- **Expert picks:** Jack of All Trades (each selection → +5% to that
  attribute until battle's end, compounding) · Flow (+1 Focus Charge
  per Stance pick) · Opportunists (all friendlies +1 Initiative)

## The synergy web — every "doubled if" pairing (verified, complete)

Thirty-six doubling clauses exist; they are the skill system's real
currency. The full map, grouped by what they build:

| Build theme | Pairings (subskill ← needs skill) |
|---|---|
| Morale engine | Resolve ← Battlecraft · Battle Frenzy (uses Morale) · Enthusiasm (self-feeds) |
| Luck engine | Beastly Confidence ← Primal Magic · Long-Sight ← Luck · Battle March ← Luck · As Luck Would Have It (uses Luck) · Chaos Reigns ← Battle Magic |
| Focus economy | Hymn to the Martyrs ← Recruitment · Battle Focus ← Battle Magic · Energizing Mana / Spellcaster Tactics (Tactics tree) · Luminous Focus (Daylight) |
| Caster stats | Battle Mage's Authority ← Sorcery · Mage Protector's Authority ← Resistance · Raw Energy ← Offense · Rhythmic Cadence ← Sorcery · Sticky Magic ← Wisdom · Manacraft ← Recruitment |
| Anti-magic | Wizard Contract ← Diplomacy · Chronic Weakness ← Resistance · Obstruction (self-scaling) · Draining/Time Shift (Resistance tree) |
| Anti-summon | Reality Wardens ← Nightshade Magic · Magic Suppression (Resistance) · Seeing Through (Wisdom) — vs. **Stabilization** (Summon Avatar), which counters all three |
| Summoner | Avatar of Fury ← Leadership · Avatar of Toughness ← Defense · Avatar of Celerity ← Insight · All Life is Endless ← Summon Avatar · Summoning Sorcery |
| Map tempo | Careful Planning ← Arcane Magic · Familiar Paths ← Wisdom · Strong Mounts ← Scouting · Speed of Light ← Logistics |
| Economy | Always Heads ← Economy · Art of the Deal ← Economy · Tax Collector ← Logistics · Experimenter ← Insight · Veterans ← Diplomacy |
| Formation | One for All ← Offense · All for One ← Defense · Elite Guards ← Tactics · Larger Than Life ← Battlecraft |
| Growth/Laws | Civic Innovation ← Leadership · Endless Charisma ← Tactics · Stargazer ← Scouting · Where the Sun Never Sets ← Daylight |

**Three verified premier stacks:**
1. **The Morale Ceiling** (from the combat appendix, now fully
   sourced): Expert Leadership (+3) + Resolve×Battlecraft (+2%/pt) +
   Knight's Honor set (+5%) + Encouragement law (+2%) + Aeos = ~20%
   extra-turn chance per point, ~100% at Morale 5.
2. **The Multi-School Archmage:** two+ school skills at Expert with
   Scholar Synergies (+6 +3 each) + Thaumaturgy double-cast + High
   Mage/Archmage (+1 level each) — every spell at max level, cast
   twice a round, at +12–15 effective Spellpower.
3. **The Unkillable Summoner:** Summon Avatar w/ Stabilization +
   Summoning Sorcery + Their Name is Legion + All Life is Endless —
   +30% bigger summons, +25% stronger, dispel-immune, and healed 30%
   harder. Schism's answer to its own counter.

## Artifact effects on the skill system (verified)

- **Relics = skill-tree cheat codes:** each Relic grants *all
  unlearned subskills* of specific trees — the Holy Sigil of Roph
  covers all six faction skills at once, deleting every
  pick-one-of-three decision above for those trees.
- **Spell-level stacking:** Fine Wand (+1 level to all spells) stacks
  with Advanced/Expert school ranks, Teachings, High Mage, and
  Archmage — the route to casting everything at L4 without ever
  finding an upgrade scroll.
- **Skill-mimics:** Fancy Mask (+2–3 battle-start Focus) is Murmuring
  in a hat · Warlord Boots (+2 army Initiative) doubles March! ·
  Ukh Signet/Tabar Signet feed Leadership/Luck scaling subskills ·
  Legion's Leg (+50% growth) multiplies Recruitment · Skull of Milos
  out-earns Basic Economy · Caduceus/Two-faced Mask extend the Combat
  tree's Heroic Strike engine.
- **Alchemical Dust loop:** Experimenter (+5 Dust/day) feeds artifact
  upgrading, which feeds the artifacts feeding your skills — the one
  closed economic loop between the two systems.
- **Spellbinder's Hat / Spells in Bottles** (all T5/T4 spells) do
  Wisdom's job as items — a Might hero with the Hat skips the entire
  magic-skill investment for access purposes.

# The Book at a Glance

The table of contents that follows is generated from the chapters
themselves; this page is the curated version — what each part is
*for*, and how complete it currently is.

## Part I — Factions (six chapters + the Matchup Grid)

One chapter per faction: identity, the faction-exclusive skill with
verified mechanics, the unit roster with full stat tables, recommended
magic schools cross-referenced to Part II's confirmed hero pairings,
hero picks, three builds, and a verdict. **Temple, Necropolis, Grove,
Hive, and Dungeon carry complete verified stat tables; Schism carries
its full roster tree and verified T5/T7 stats** (its remaining rows
are the book's one persistent sourcing gap — the official page resists
retrieval). The Matchup Grid closes the part with all thirty pairings
rated and the three structural rules they obey.

## Part II — Magic (five schools, 83 spells)

The book's deepest section, and the one whose format everything else
inherits. Every spell: plain English, the exact formula worked at all
four levels, an "In battle" example against the reference army, the
Masterful version and its owning hero where one exists, and a
strategy layer with a verdict. Eleven Masterful hero pairings are
confirmed from official sources, two near-confirmed, nine tracked as
open. The Neutral Magic chapter also documents the level-gate and
points systems unique to that school.

## Part III — Heroes, Skills, Artifacts, Creatures

- **Skills:** the full 30-skill taxonomy, the rank-and-subskill fork
  system, a complete worked Offense tree, and the synergy-clause
  build philosophy. The six faction skills live in their faction
  chapters; the four school skills in their magic chapters.
- **Artifacts:** the 14-slot system, rarities, upgrading, set
  mechanics with both verified flagship sets, slot-by-slot standouts,
  and four assembled build packages.
- **Creatures:** the neutral bestiary — 21 creatures across 8 tiers,
  acquisition rules, and how to fight (or recruit) the map's own army,
  up to the Tier-8 Lich Dragon.
- **Heroes:** hero-class and per-hero coverage beyond the pairings
  already embedded in Parts I–II is the roadmap's next major pass.

## Appendices

**Combat Mechanics** — the verified damage pipeline, morale/luck
math, the creature-type table (including exactly what feeds
Necromancy), and the documented stacking ceilings. The ten most
load-bearing pages in the book; everything else's shorthand resolves
here.

## What "complete" means in Early Access

Every chapter footer carries a retrieval date, and every category's
source folder carries an `_audit.md` ledger of verified content versus
known gaps. This book's standing rule: **a tracked gap over an
invented number, every time.** When the game leaves Early Access and
the remaining wiki pages fill in, the audits are the checklist for the
final edition.
